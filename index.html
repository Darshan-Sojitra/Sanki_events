Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our ticketing service

Purchasing tickets is simple! Browse our events, select the one you're interested in, click "Get Tickets," and follow the checkout process. You can pay using credit/debit cards, PayPal, or other supported payment methods.

Our refund policy varies by event. Generally, you can request a refund up to 7 days before the event date. Some events may have specific policies set by the organizers. Check the event details page for specific refund information.

After purchase, your tickets will be emailed to you as e-tickets with QR codes. You can also access them in your account under "My Tickets." Simply show the e-ticket on your phone or print it out to enter the event.

Yes, there may be service fees and processing charges added to the ticket price. These fees help us maintain our platform and provide customer support. All fees will be clearly displayed before you complete your purchase.

Yes, you can transfer tickets to friends or family. In your account, go to "My Tickets," select the tickets you want to transfer, and enter the recipient's email address. They'll receive instructions on how to claim the tickets.
